⛳ Buddy Group

Tee Sheet Builder

Set up the course and the foursomes, then print scorecards.

1 The round

Prints at the top of every scorecard.

2 The course

Enter Par and Stroke Index (the hole's handicap, 1–18) for each hole — copy these straight off the course's own scorecard. Yards is optional. You only do this once per course; it's saved.

3 Players & groups

Add each player with the Course Handicap from GHIN, then put them in a group. On the scorecard, a dot is printed on every hole where that player gets a stroke.

4 Tee times (optional)

A time or starting hole per group, if you want it on the card.

5 Print

Scorecards print one group per page in landscape. In the print screen pick a printer or Save as PDF.

6 Backup (works anywhere)

Tap Copy backup, paste it into Notes. Paste it back here later and tap Restore to bring the course and roster back on any device.
