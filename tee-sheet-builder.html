Tee Sheet Builder

Type the foursomes once, print cart signs and a pairing sheet.

1 The round

Shows up at the top of every cart sign and the pairing sheet.

2 Players

Pull each Course Handicap from the GHIN app's handicap calculator, then assign a group. Strokes are figured automatically off the lowest handicap in each group.

PlayerCourse HCPGroup

3 Tee times (optional)

Add a time or starting hole per group. Leave blank if you don't need them.

4 Print

Both open your browser's print dialog — choose "Save as PDF" to keep a copy, or send straight to a printer. Cart signs print two to a page; the pairing sheet is one page.

5 Backup (works anywhere)

Tap Copy backup, then paste into Notes or an email to yourself. Next week, paste it back here and tap Restore — the whole roster comes back, even on a different iPad.
